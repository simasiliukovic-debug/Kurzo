How to Get the Content Creator Role

Welcome! If you want to get the Content Creator role in our server, follow this detailed guide. Make sure to provide a screenshot as proof that you own your social media account. The screenshot must be taken in YouTube Studio for YouTube, YouTube Shorts or TikTok, it must clearly show your account statistics.

Important: We only provide verification for YouTube, YouTube Shorts, and TikTok. Other platforms are not eligible for this role.

Requirements

  • YouTube channels must have at least 1,000 subscribers.
  • TikTok accounts must have at least 50,000 likes.
  • Your channel or account must be active and have visible content.
  • Only accounts that clearly show ownership can be approved.

Step-by-Step Instructions

For YouTube / YouTube Shorts

  1. Open YouTube Studio: Go to YouTube Studio and log in.
  2. Prepare Your Screenshot: Take a clear screenshot showing your channel/account name, subscriber count, profile picture, and verification elements.
  3. Send Your Screenshot: DM a server moderator with your screenshot attached and include your channel name.
  4. Wait for Review: Moderators will review your submission (up to 24 hours).
  5. Receive Your Role: Once approved, you will get the Content Creator role automatically.

For TikTok

  1. Open TikTok: Log in to your TikTok account on the app or web browser and go to your profile.
  2. Access Analytics: Tap the three dots (or settings icon), go to Creator Tools, then select Analytics.
  3. Prepare Your Screenshot: Take a clear screenshot showing your TikTok username, total likes (must be 50k+), profile picture, and other visible stats that prove ownership.
  4. Send Your Screenshot: DM a server moderator with your screenshot attached and include your TikTok username.
  5. Wait for Review: Moderators will review your submission (up to 24 hours).
  6. Receive Your Role: Once approved, you will get the Content Creator role automatically.

Tip: Double-check that all important details are visible in your screenshot. If your screenshot is blurry or missing information, your request may be delayed or rejected.

Additional Notes

  • If your account is newly created, wait at least 7 days or it needs to meet the Requirements before submitting.
  • Make sure your social media account is public so moderators can verify your account.
  • After receiving the Content Creator role, you can access exclusive channels, events, and special perks.
  • Keep your DMs open so moderators can contact you if they need more information.
